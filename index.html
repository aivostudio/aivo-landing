FAQ

A few quick answers before you start generating.

What is a credit?

A credit is a unit you spend when generating a new video. Longer videos and higher resolutions use more credits.

Can I use AIVO videos commercially?

Yes. You can use AIVO videos in your own music videos, ads, streams and social content. Just make sure you own the music rights.

Do I need any editing skills?

No. AIVO is designed so that artists and creators can generate content without touching a timeline or keyframes.

Will there be a free trial?

We plan to offer free starter credits during launch so you can test the platform before buying larger packs.
